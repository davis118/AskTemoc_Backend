University Commons
Items To Bring 
In University Commons, mattresses are Twin XL.
In Canyon Creek Heights North, mattresses are Twin XL.
In Canyon Creek Heights South, mattresses are full-sized.
Bed linens.
Pillow.
Towels and washcloths.
Toiletries (soap, shampoo, hand soap, deodorant, toothbrush, toothpaste, etc.).
Blow dryer.
Laundry detergent.
Laundry bag.
Trash bags.
Cleaning supplies.
Clothes hangers.
Toilet paper.
Swimsuit. (There are pools, but no lifeguards on duty.)
Basketball or other sports gear.
Coffee pot with automatic shut off.
Canyon Creek Heights
Items Not to Bring 
Candles.
Toasters.
Hot plates.
Weapons.
Drugs/illegal substances.
Pets.
Tobacco. UT Dallas is a tobacco free campus  .
For a full list of prohibited items, see the Resident Handbook.
