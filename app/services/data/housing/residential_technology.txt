Residential Technology

University Housing provides residents with wireless and wired internet through Boldyn.

Get Connected

Step 1: Download Duo Mobile

Download the Duo Mobile Authentication App on your phone.

Step 2: Access the Online Portal

If connecting for the first time, open your wireless settings and connect to “MyResNet Start Here”.
If reconnecting, go to Boldyn’s online portal.

Step 3: Sign In

Sign into the portal using your UT Dallas email address and password.
Accept the Duo notification push.
Accept Boldyn’s terms and complete your profile.
Note the Wi-Fi passkey that pops up on your screen.
To change your password, click “show Wi-Fi password” under the “Add Devices” tab. This is a private password and should not be shared.

Step 4: Connect to the Network

Open your wireless settings on each device you are connecting.
Connect to MyResNet-5G. (Only connect to MyResNet-2G if you cannot see the 5G network.)
Enter your Wi-Fi password when prompted.
Enable the “auto-join” feature.

Step 5: Connecting Other Devices

For a wired device, or a device without a graphical user interface (GUI), login to the portal and manually add the MAC address in the My Devices tab.

Guest Network

Guests and visitors can connect to the guest network for up to 12 hours.

Step 1: From your device, click on “MyResNet-Start-Here” in your available networks. If the network is not available, go to dallasoncampus.hed.boldyn.com

Step 2: In the new portal, click “Guests and Visitors”.

Step 3: Click “Continue”.

Step 4: Check the box to agree to the terms, then click “Create Guest Account”.
