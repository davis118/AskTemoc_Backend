Floorplans

All measurements are approximate and may vary slightly from one unit to another. If you are unsure whether an item will fit, we suggest waiting until you move in and take measurements yourself. Please note that we are unable to show incoming residents their rooms prior to their assigned move-in day due to our maintenance and housekeeping operations. Before you go shopping, remember to check out our “What to Bring/Not to Bring” page to see what is allowed.
