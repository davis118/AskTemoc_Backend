Corporate interns and summer guests have two housing options: University Commons (our most centrally-located and highly-requested community), or Canyon Creek Heights.

University Commons
University Commons – Furnished Residence Halls

University Commons residence halls feature three-bedroom suites, recreation areas, large communal spaces and study lounges.

Canyon Creek Heights
Canyon Creek Heights – Furnished Apartments

Canyon Creek Heights apartments offer a variety of floor plans and amenities.
