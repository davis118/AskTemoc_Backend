Housing Agreements

All housing agreements are for the complete academic year.

Students who live in University Commons will sign an academic year agreement (mid-August through mid-May), with an option to participate in summer housing if desired.

All housing fees associated with assignments in these buildings will be processed through the Bursar Office. Housing fees for the full housing agreement period will be divided into two equal charges. Your University account will be charged once prior to the fall semester and once prior to the spring semester, in accordance with Bursar timelines. Summer charges for University Commons will be in addition to the listed rates.

Students who move on campus prior to census day will be charged the full amount of housing for the semester. Charges will not be pro-rated until these dates:

Spring 2026: Feb. 4
Summer 2026: June 16
Fall 2026: Sept. 9
Spring 2027: Feb. 3
