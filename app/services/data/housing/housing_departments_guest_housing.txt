The University of Texas at Dallas welcomes interns and researchers traveling to the Dallas Fort-Worth area to live on a safe, modern and amenity-filled campus each summer. With multiple floorplans available, UT Dallas offers competitive pricing for the metroplex and its location in Richardson, Texas, is minutes away from Texas Instruments, Raytheon, State Farm and many more large corporations.

We encourage anyone interested in applying to do so following a review of our pricing and policies pages. Any questions about the program or our offerings should be sent to housing@utdallas.edu.

2026 Availability

The 2026 Corporate Intern Application will open on Monday, Jan. 12, 2026 at 10 a.m. Guest Housing for Summer 2026 will be available from Jun. 1 – Aug. 2, 2026.

Timeline
Jan. 12, 2026: 2026 Corporate Intern Application opens. To select a preferred roommate, all preferred roommates must email housing@utdallas.edu and include the following information:
Name(s) of all preferred roommates.
A written request to be paired with the names listed in the email.
Apr. 14, 2026: 2026 Corporate Intern Application closes.
Apr. 15, 2026: Final day to cancel your 2026 Corporate Intern reservation. Interns must cancel their reservation within the application portal and also submit a formal written request to housing@utdallas.edu. Cancellation for any reason after Apr. 15, 2026 will result in forfeiture of all payments received.
Apr. 17, 2026: Intern Offers sent. Applicants will receive an email with their assigned room space and the date by which they must accept or decline the offer.
Apr. 30, 2026: Check-In and Move-In instructions will be sent to all interns who have accepted their offer.
June. 1, 2026: 2026 Corporate Intern Move-In.
July 1-15. 2026: 2026 Corporate Intern 2025 Move-Out instructions will be sent to all interns.
Aug. 2, 2026: 2026 Corporate Intern Move-Out day. All interns, unless given special permission, are required to be moved out by 7 p.m. on Aug. 2, 2026.
