$100/person
Parking

Parking permits are purchased directly from the Parking & Transportation Office. Email park@utdallas.edu with further questions.

There is a seven night minimum requirement for all accommodations.
A $300 service charge will be added to any stay totaling 30 days or less.
Payment is due in full in advance or at check in. Payment may be made by check, money order, or credit card.
Cancellations made by April 15 will receive a full refund of the housing charges paid in advance. Cancellations made for any reason after April 15 will result in forfeiture of all payments received.

No refunds will be provided after occupancy is taken, including for unused meals, parking, and Activity Center membership.
Rooms will be checked after departure. A final bill may be issued for any damages incurred.
Linens are not included. Please familiarize yourself with what to bring.
