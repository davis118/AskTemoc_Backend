Staff Listings

The University Housing leadership staff provides oversight for all campus housing. UT Dallas departments should contact a member of the University Housing team listed below. Residents who feel their needs have not been addressed by University Housing staff can also select from this listing.

Mission Statement

University Housing strives to provide an exceptional residential experience by delivering educational opportunities and supportive services that foster diverse engagement, individual growth and community belonging.
