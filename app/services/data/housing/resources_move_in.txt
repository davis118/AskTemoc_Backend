Fall 2025 Move-In Dates

Monday 8/18: Andromeda Hall, Sirius Hall, Capella Hall
Tuesday 8/19: Andromeda Hall, Sirius Hall, Capella Hall
Wednesday 8/20: Helix Hall, Vega Hall, Canyon Creek Heights North
Thursday 8/21: Helix Hall, Vega Hall, Canyon Creek Heights South
Saturday 8/23: University Village

Housing assignments will be sent to the students’ UT Dallas email accounts the first week in July. Emails to first-year University Commons residents will include a link to sign up for their preferred move-in date and time. University Village and Canyon Creek Heights residents will receive a pre-assigned move-in date and time.
